About Me

I am broadly interested in the intersection of Control theory and Robotics. I have a dual M.Sc. degree in Control Engineering and Mechatronics Engineering both from Tabriz, Iran. Much of my M.Sc. thesis is focused on Port-Hamiltonian systems and IDA-PBC which is done in University of Naples, Federico II. Importantly, I largely focus on Robust Control and Disturbance Attuniation.
